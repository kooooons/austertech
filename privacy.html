Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains how Auster collects and uses your personal data when you visit austertech.dk or use our contact form.

Who we are

Auster is a web design and hosting company based in Denmark. You can reach us at contact@austertech.dk.

What data we collect

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. We do not collect any other personal data, and we do not use cookies or tracking technologies.

How we use your data

We use your contact details solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not sell, share, or pass on your data to third parties, and we do not use it for marketing purposes unless you explicitly ask us to stay in touch.

How long we keep it

We keep your message for as long as necessary to handle your enquiry and any follow-up, after which it is deleted.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, email us at contact@austertech.dk.

Hosting & infrastructure

This website is hosted on Cloudflare's global network. Cloudflare may process technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of delivering the site. You can read Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.
